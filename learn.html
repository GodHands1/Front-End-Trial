Frequently Asked Questions

What is cryptocurrency?

+

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security. It operates independently of a central bank and uses blockchain technology to record transactions.

How do I start investing in cryptocurrency?

+

Start by creating an account on CryPIto, complete the verification process, deposit funds, and begin with small investments in established cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin or Ethereum.

Is cryptocurrency safe?

+

Cryptocurrency can be safe when proper security measures are followed. Use reputable exchanges, enable two-factor authentication, and store your assets in secure wallets.

What is blockchain technology?

+

Blockchain is a distributed ledger technology that maintains a continuously growing list of records, called blocks, which are linked and secured using cryptography.

How are cryptocurrency prices determined?

+

Cryptocurrency prices are determined by supply and demand, market sentiment, adoption rates, regulatory news, and overall market conditions.
